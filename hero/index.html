How to Play

Welcome to Element Heroes. In this game, you build element combos, use reactions, and attack your opponent using chemistry-based strategy.

1. Build your field
Play element cards like Oxygen, Water, Iron, Hydrogen, or Sodium onto your field.
2. Trigger reactions
Reaction cards only work when the correct elements are already on your field.
3. Use attacks wisely
Some attacks become stronger if the enemy is Wet or Corroded.
4. Watch your statuses
Wet and Corroded can change what happens each turn.
5. Win the match
Reduce your opponent’s HP to 0 before they defeat you.
Tip: You can only keep up to 3 element cards on your field, so choose your setup carefully.
