Welcome to Element Heroes
Element Heroes is a 2-player chemistry battle game. You place substance cards on your field, use reaction cards when the correct required cards are ready, and attack your opponent with smart combos. To win, reduce the other player's HP to 0 before they defeat you.

How a Turn Works
A simple guide for your first match
1
Build your field

Play substance cards like Oxygen, Water, Iron, Hydrogen, Sodium, or Calcium onto your field.

2
Check your energy

Each card has an energy cost. You need enough energy to play it.

3
Use reactions

Reaction cards only work when the required cards are already on your field.

4
Attack smartly

Some attacks become stronger if the enemy is Wet or Corroded.

5
End your turn

When you are done, click End Turn so your opponent can play.

Important Rules
These rules matter a lot during the game
A
You can only keep 3 substance cards on your field

Choose carefully. Your field setup decides which reactions you can use.

B
Reactions fail without the right cards

If the required cards are not on your field, the reaction card cannot be used.

C
Status effects change the battle

Wet can make some attacks stronger. Corroded causes damage over time.

D
Utility cards help your side

Catalyst gives extra energy. Lab Shield heals HP.

E
Plan ahead

Strong players do not just play random cards. They build a combo first.

Card Types
Every card belongs to one of these groups
E

Substance

These are the building blocks of the game. Put them on your field to unlock reactions.

R

Reaction

These cards represent chemistry reactions. They need the correct required cards already on your field.

A

Attack

These deal direct damage and can become stronger when the right statuses or elements are in play.

U

Utility

These help your side with energy gain, healing, and support.

Strategy Tips
Build your field first, then use the reaction card when the combo is ready. Try to notice when your opponent is Wet or Corroded, because that can make some cards much stronger. Keep your field slots open for the elements you really need instead of filling all 3 spots too early.
